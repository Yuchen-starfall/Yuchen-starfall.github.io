## DNA folding VS. DNA origami

### DNA origami

**DNA origami** is the folding of DNA into nanoscale two- and three-dimensional structures using specific base pairing to create desired shapes. 

-- Paul W.K. el.al (2006) Nature.    (the first demonstration of DNA origami )

![Test](img/img-DNA_folding/1.png)

### DNA folding

* Chromosome, chromatid,chromatin, and nucleosomes

* **DNA folding** is an active and well-controlled process by which the DNA double helix is hierarchically organized into higher-order structures, servingbothstructuralandregulatoryfunctions.

## Chromosome Territory（染色质疆域）

DNA-DNA contacts predominantly happen within particular chromosomes.（其本质均交联离得近的DNA- DNA或DNA-蛋白，之后运用交联后无法被酶切的特性，对相互交联的位置进行测序）

![Test](/img/img-DNA_folding/6.png)

* Chromosome Conformation Capture (3C)

* Chromatin immunoprecipitation sequencing (ChIP-seq)

Hi-C maps -- Topologically Associated Domains or TADs

![Test](/img/img-DNA_folding/7.png)

![Test](/img/img-DNA_folding/8.png)

一些蛋白质目前被认为对拓扑结构域的形成有关，其中包括转录因子CTCF和蛋白质复合体黏连蛋白 (cohesin)。这些蛋白通过染色质免疫共沉淀测序技术被发现富集在拓扑结构域的边界上。由于边界还富集有大量常表达基因 (housekeeping gene) 和转运 RNA (tRNA) 基因，一种理论认为活跃的转录与拓扑结构域的形成有关。新的理论认为，拓扑结构域可以通过 DNA 环外挤 (DNA loop extrusion) 机制得以实现。这一理论预测黏连蛋白在 DNA 上滑动，直到遇到CTCF二聚体之后停止运动。
